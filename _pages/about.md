# Chasing the Sun & Good Food.

![Emily enjoying a sunny day](../media/about-portrait.webp)

Hi, I’m Emily. 

If I had to describe my life in a color, it would be golden-hour yellow. 

When I packed up my life and moved to Los Angeles a few years ago, I was immediately struck by the light. Everything here feels just a little bit brighter, a little more sun-drenched. But more than the endless summer weather, I fell head over heels for the food scene—specifically, the weekend rituals at the local farmers' markets. 

There is nothing quite like strolling through the stalls on a hazy Sunday morning, cold brew in hand, surrounded by the most vibrant, fragrant produce you've ever seen. That’s where my obsession with fresh citrus truly began. From sweet Meyer lemons to ruby red grapefruits, I started bringing home bags of the brightest fruits I could find and experimenting in my sunlit kitchen. 

**Lemonade Days** was born out of that exact feeling. It’s a digital recipe box and a love letter to the retro LA summer aesthetic. Here, you'll find laid-back, fresh recipes that don't take themselves too seriously. Cooking should feel like a breeze, just like a drive down the Pacific Coast Highway with the windows rolled down. 

Grab a cold glass of something sweet, stay a while, and let's squeeze the day.
